Who are the Elemental Dragons?

We are a team of high school students who annually compete in the FIRST Robotics Competition. We build robots to compete in these competitions. Throughout this process, team members develop skills in mechanical engineering, electrical engineering, and programming.

Who can join?

For the main team, membership is open to all Iredell County High School Students, including all students in the Iredell Statesville School District.

Who are the Elemental Dragons?

We are a team of high school students in Iredell County, NC who build robots annually compete in the FIRST Robotics Competition. This competition provides members with real world experience and practical knowledge with robotics. When we aren't competing, we work toward providing quailty STEM education to the youth of out local community.

Who can join?

For the main team, membership is open to all Iredell County High School Students, including all students in the Iredell Statesville School District.

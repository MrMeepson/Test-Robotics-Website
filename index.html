Who are we?

We are the Elemental Dragons, FRC Team 3680, a collection of high school students designing, building, programming, and competing in Robotics. On top of that, we also place a hugh emphasis on community service and try to make STEM education more accessable. While on the team, members learn engineering skills, practice digital media, obtain real world hands on experiences, develop business frameworks, service communities with STEM education, market, and have fun.

Who can join?

For the main team, membership is open to all Iredell County High School Students, including all students in the Iredell Statesville School District.
